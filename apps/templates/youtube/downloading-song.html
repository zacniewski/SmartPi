Is it legal?

Short answer: Yes. You aren’t allowed to download material that is copyrighted if you do not have express permission. If the content is not copyright, then you are allowed to download or "rip" both the audio and video from YouTube uploads. Ripping audio content from videos uploaded on YouTube breaks YouTube’s terms of service (TOS). There’s a big difference between US copyright laws and YouTube’s TOS. Violating YouTube’s terms of service is not illegal by law but YouTube could ban your account.
